# GENESIS Proposal — AI-Driven Materials Discovery

## Overview

Proposal development around AI for materials discovery and design. Centered on linking microstructure and mechanical behavior through coupled latent models, uncertainty-aware representation learning, and generative methods for heterogeneous materials. PBX is the motivating system, but the framing is broader: AI systems that can represent, predict, and design materials with targeted functionality.

## Status
