# Yield Surface Reconstruction

## Overview

Reconstructing or learning yield surfaces from data, simulations, or constitutive structure. Represent complex material response surfaces in a way that is physically meaningful and useful for downstream modeling and analysis.

## Status

Active research project.

## Key Goals

- Define the yield-surface representation
- Determine whether the reconstruction is analytic, data-driven, or ML-assisted
- Validate against known constitutive behavior
- Make it useful for downstream mechanics or constitutive modeling tasks

No fixed external deadline.
