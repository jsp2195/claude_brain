# Microstructure-to-Response Modeling for Heterogeneous Materials

## Overview

Broader research direction focused on learning the relationship between material microstructure and mechanical response. Connect representation learning and generative modeling to constitutive behavior, response prediction, and eventually inverse design.

## Status

Active conceptual and technical development. Both a live research thread and an umbrella direction connecting several subprojects.

## Key Goals

- Build physically meaningful latent representations
- Connect graph-based microstructure encodings to response variables or response manifolds
- Develop a modeling framework that can both predict behavior from structure and help generate structures for target behavior

## Deadlines

No fixed external deadline. Central medium-term research direction that feeds directly into proposal work.
